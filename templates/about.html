About Skin Disease Detection

Skin Disease Detection is an AI-driven platform designed to assist in the early detection of dermatological conditions using deep learning and image analysis. Our goal is to bridge the gap between users and timely dermatological assessments, especially in remote or underserved areas.

Our Mission

We aim to democratize access to skin healthcare by providing a smart, accurate, and easy-to-use solution that empowers users to take proactive steps in understanding their skin health.

Key Features

  • Upload an image of a skin condition and get instant AI-powered predictions
  • Integrated chatbot offering information on symptoms, treatment, and prevention
  • Secure prediction history stored for future reference

Our Team

We are a team of passionate developers, researchers, and healthcare enthusiasts committed to leveraging AI for social good. Our combined expertise in machine learning, software engineering, and user experience allows us to deliver reliable and meaningful solutions.
